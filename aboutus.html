Our Journey

Founded in 2017, ServPro Consolidated Inc. has grown from a small idea to an industry leader, fueled by innovation and a commitment to excellence.
We are proud of our progress and excited for the future ahead.

2017

Establishment of ServPro

Founded ServPro with a vision to provide exceptional HVAC services to both residential and commercial clients.

2018

First Accreditation

Achieved our first industry accreditation, establishing ServPro as a certified and trusted provider in the HVAC sector.

2019

Establishment of Travel and Tours Service

Expanded our business portfolio by launching a dedicated Travel and Tours service, offering comprehensive travel solutions.

2020

First Big Project in Palawan

Successfully completed our first major project in Palawan, showcasing our capability to handle large-scale installations in remote locations.

2021

Moved to a New Location

Relocated to a larger facility to accommodate our growing team and expanding operations.

2024

Chilled Water System Project

Implemented an innovative chilled water system project, demonstrating our technical expertise and commitment to energy-efficient solutions.
