TRAVEL TIPS & GUIDES

Essential information for your journey

Best Time to Visit

  • Dry Season: December to May
  • Peak Season: March to May
  • Monsoon: June to November
  • Best Deals: June to September

Budget Planning

  • Budget: ₱2,000-3,000/day
  • Mid-range: ₱4,000-6,000/day
  • Luxury: ₱8,000+/day
  • Transportation: ₱500-1,000/day

Packing Essentials

  • Light, breathable clothing
  • Sun protection
  • Insect repellent
  • Universal adapter

Local Customs

  • Respectful dress code
  • Remove shoes indoors
  • Elder respect: "Po/Opo"
  • Tipping: 10-15%
